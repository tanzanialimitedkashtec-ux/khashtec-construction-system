About KASHTEC

KASHTEC Tanzania Limited, incorporated on 19th September 2022, is a multidisciplinary and fast-growing construction company dedicated to serving as a one-stop center for Building, Civil Works Contracting, and Real Estate Development projects.

Vision

To become an effective and competent partner in fostering the construction industry in Tanzania and provide world class services.

Mission

  • Offer comprehensive solutions in real estate, building and civil works.
  • Provide expert project and claims management support.
  • Ensure efficiency, cost-effectiveness and excellence.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Quality
  • Innovation
  • Professionalism
  • Sustainability

Our Services

  • Design and Build of Residential & Commercial Buildings
  • Civil Works (Roads, Bridges, Culverts)
  • Real Estate Development
  • Construction Claims Management
  • Professional Training
  • Petroleum Tank Installation
  • Telecommunication Towers Installation

Major Projects

Dar es Salaam Port Modernization Project

Subcontract works under DP World – EDECS Engineering including manpower supply, concrete pavement works and equipment rental.

Residential Buildings – Kinondoni & Goba

Main and sub-contractor roles in residential building developments in Dar es Salaam.

Fukayosi Real Estate Project – Bagamoyo

Commercial & Residential Real Estate Project under KASHTEC Estate Projects.
